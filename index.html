What is Inner Peace and Zen?

Inner Peace and Zen is a place for you to relax, unwind, and declutter your mind. You are able to write down your thoughts for the day, track your progress for your goals, track your quality of sleep, and find resources to aid you in your mental health and self improvement journey.

Features

Journal

Write down your thoughts for the day, overall mood, sleep quality, and updates on your physical health to help you unload and free your mind.

Resources

Find resources to aid you in your mental health and self improvement journey. Find books, articles, and videos that can help you in any kind of way.

Community

Connect with others and talk about your journeys. Share your goals and your accomplishments. Find new friends or simply share your thoughts.
